Doesn't Work? We Replace It.

If any product — seed, insecticide, herbicide, or fungicide — doesn't perform as committed despite correct usage, we replace it free of charge. Next-day delivery. Covers every product in our catalogue.

Replacement process

1.Report the issue via app, WhatsApp, or this form.
2.Our team reviews and calls you within 4 hours.
3.Replacement dispatched and delivered next day.

What's covered

✅ Seeds with germination below commitment
✅ Insecticide with less than committed pest control
✅ Herbicide with less than committed weed suppression

FAQ

Common Questions About Rythu Mitra Suraksha

How is Rythu Mitra Suraksha different from a money-back guarantee?

A money-back guarantee pays you after your crop fails. Rythu Mitra Suraksha works before and during the season — verifying your product is authentic before you sow, giving you a yield target to aim for, and replacing any underperforming product mid-season so your crop still has a chance.

How long does a replacement take?

Once your replacement request is approved within 4 hours of submission, the replacement product is dispatched and delivered the next day to your home or village pickup point.

Does replacement cover all products?

Yes. Seeds, insecticides, herbicides, and fungicides — every product in the Rythu Mitra catalogue is covered by the free replacement policy.

What if I scan the QR and it shows "not verified"?

Do not use the product. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp with a photo of the product and QR code. We will investigate and replace the product within 24 hours at no cost.

Do I need to return the product to get a replacement?

No return needed. A photo of the issue and your order number is all we need to process your replacement request.

Can I request a replacement more than once in a season?

Yes. Each product in your order is covered independently. If multiple products underperform, you can request replacement for each one separately.
