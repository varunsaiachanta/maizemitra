Frequently Asked Questions
How does the crop guarantee work? ∨
Follow our guidelines, if the crop fails due to product issues, we inspect and refund fully within 7 days.
What photos do I need for a claim? ∨
Seed bag before sowing, field after sowing, crop at 20 days, and problem area with visible date.
How long does delivery take? ∨
Same day or next day within the village. You get a notification when out for delivery.
Can I pay cash on delivery? ∨
Yes — we accept Cash on Delivery and UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm).
Are the products genuine? ∨
Every product is sourced directly from licensed manufacturers. We provide batch traceability for all items.
