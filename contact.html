Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you receive a WhatsApp confirmation with a tracking link. You can also follow updates in the Rythu Mitra app under My Orders.

What if I receive a damaged product?

Take a photo immediately and send it on WhatsApp or through the replacement form. Damaged delivery cases are covered by free replacement.

How do I verify a product before sowing?

Use the scan flow inside Rythu Mitra Suraksha. It shows the batch number, licence details, expiry, and lab-test summary before the pack is opened.

Can I get dosage help for a product?

Yes. Call us or send a WhatsApp photo of the label and we will explain the timing, dosage, and application method clearly.

Crop Emergency?

If you see sudden wilting, pest outbreak, or unusual spots, send us a field photo right now and we will guide you on the next step.
