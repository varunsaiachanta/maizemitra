Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our farmers. Can't find your answer? WhatsApp us directly.

How do I track my order?+
Once your order is placed, you will receive a WhatsApp confirmation with a tracking link. You can also check order status in the MaizeMitra app under "My Orders". Delivery updates are sent automatically at each stage.
Can I cancel my order?+
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placing them before they are packed. After packing begins, cancellation is not possible but we accept returns of unopened products within 48 hours of delivery.
What if I receive a damaged product?+
Take a photo of the damaged product immediately and send it to our WhatsApp. We replace or refund within 48 hours, no questions asked. This includes leaking bottles, torn seed bags, or broken seals.
I can't read the dosage instructions. Can you help?+
Absolutely. Call us or send a WhatsApp photo of the product and we'll walk you through the exact dosage, timing, and application method verbally or via voice note. We also have video guides in Hindi and Marathi.
How do I submit crop photos for the guarantee?+
You can submit crop photos three ways: through the MaizeMitra app (Photos section), via WhatsApp to our support number, or by emailing them to support@kisandirect.in. Include your order number in the message.
Do you deliver outside Nandgaon village?+
Currently we are in a pilot phase serving Nandgaon village only. We are expanding to 4 neighbouring villages in Q3 2025. Contact us with your village name and we'll add you to our early access list for your area.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept Cash on Delivery (pay when you receive your order), UPI payments (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfers. We do not accept credit/debit card payments at this time.
Can I get an invoice for my purchase?+
Yes. A GST invoice is generated automatically for every order and sent to your WhatsApp number. You can also download it from the app under "My Orders → Invoice". Physical copies are available on request.
