Our Story

Built for the Farmer
Who Was Let Down

KisanDirect was born from a simple, painful observation: the people who feed the nation are the least protected in the supply chain. We're here to change that.

The Problem We Witnessed

Where It Started

In the summer of 2023, during a visit to Nandgaon village, we met Ramesh — a farmer who had just lost his entire 4-acre maize crop. He had spent ₹12,000 on seeds and pesticides from the local market. Both turned out to be counterfeit.

Ramesh had done everything right. He sowed on time, followed the spacing instructions, watered consistently. But the seeds never germinated properly. When he complained to the dealer, the response was a shrug and a door slammed in his face.

"I lost 6 months of work and ₹12,000 in one season. No one helped me. No one was responsible."
— Ramesh Kumar, Nandgaon Village

Ramesh's story isn't unique. It's the story of millions of small farmers across India every single Kharif and Rabi season. We decided that had to change.

How We Operate

Our Core Values

🛡️
Accountability First
We back every product we sell with a money-back guarantee. This isn't a marketing promise — it's a legal commitment. We built our business model around this accountability from day one.
🔍
Radical Transparency
We publish exactly what our guarantee covers and what it doesn't. We list the manufacturer name on every product. We tell farmers what dosage to use and what to expect. No fine print tricks.
🤝
Farmer-First Decisions
Every feature, pricing decision, and policy is evaluated by one test: does this make the farmer's life better? If a business decision conflicts with farmer benefit, the farmer wins.
📚
Knowledge as Power
We don't just sell products — we transfer knowledge. Usage guides, spray schedules, crop calendars, and 24-hour support ensure our products are used correctly every time.
🌱
Sustainable Growth
We grow one village at a time, proving the model before scaling. We'd rather serve 50 farmers exceptionally well than 5,000 farmers poorly.
🏭
Direct Supply Integrity
We source exclusively from licensed manufacturers with verified batch records. Our supply chain has exactly two steps: factory and farmer. No room for counterfeits.
