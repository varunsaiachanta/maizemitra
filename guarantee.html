The Process

How the Guarantee Works

Three simple steps protect your entire crop investment from the moment you order to the day you harvest.

1

Order & Follow Our Guidelines

Every product comes with a printed farming guide. Follow our recommended seed rate, spacing, sowing time, and pesticide schedule. These are your guarantee conditions.

📏Seed rate: 8–10 kg per acre
📐Spacing: 60 cm × 20 cm
📅Sow during June–July (Kharif)
2

Submit Crop Photos at 3 Stages

Take photos at three key crop stages and upload them via our app or WhatsApp. This creates your proof record and keeps your guarantee active throughout the season.

📸Photo 1: Seed bag before sowing (Day 0)
📸Photo 2: Field after sowing (Day 7)
📸Photo 3: Crop at vegetative stage (Day 20–25)
3

Raise a Claim — Refund in 7 Days

If your crop fails due to a product issue, raise a claim via the app, WhatsApp, or phone. Our team reviews your photos and visits the field if needed. Approved refunds are processed within 7 days.

📋Claim review within 48 hours
🔍Field inspection if required
💰Full refund to your UPI / bank account

Scope

What's Covered & What's Not

Being transparent is core to how we build trust. Here is exactly what our guarantee covers and what falls outside its scope.

✅

Guarantee Covers

🌱
Germination failure below 70% when sown at recommended rate, spacing, and time.
🐛
Pesticide ineffectiveness on the target pest when applied at correct dosage and timing.
🌿
Herbicide failure to control the specified weeds when applied as directed on the product label.
📦
Expired or defective products supplied by KisanDirect, including leaking bottles or damaged seed bags.
💰
Full purchase price refunded if claim is approved after inspection within 7 working days.
❌

Not Covered

🌧️
Natural disasters — floods, drought, hailstorm, or extreme weather events that damage the crop.
📋
Non-compliance with guidelines — wrong seed rate, incorrect spacing, out-of-season sowing.
📸
No photo documentation — claims without submitted crop photos at all 3 required stages.
🕐
Late claims — claims raised more than 30 days after visible crop failure symptoms first appeared.
🌍
Soil or land issues — crop failures attributable to known poor soil quality, waterlogging, or salinity.

FAQ

Common Questions
About the Guarantee

Can't find your answer here? Contact our support team — we respond within 2 hours.

How long does the refund take after approval?+
Once your claim is approved, we process the refund to your UPI ID or bank account within 7 working days. You will receive a confirmation message once payment is initiated.
Do I need to visit an office to file a claim?+
No office visit needed. You can file the claim entirely through the KisanDirect app, WhatsApp, or this website. If a field inspection is required, our agent will come to your farm — you don't need to go anywhere.
What if only part of my crop failed?+
Partial crop failures are assessed proportionally. If 40% of your field failed due to a product issue and 60% grew normally, the claim would cover approximately 40% of the relevant product cost. Our inspection team will determine the affected area.
I forgot to submit the Day 7 photo. Can I still claim?+
Missing one photo makes the claim harder to approve but not automatically invalid. Contact our support team as soon as possible to discuss your situation. We review each case individually with fairness.
Can I claim for multiple products in one order?+
Yes. If multiple products from one order contributed to the crop failure, you can include all of them in a single claim. Each product will be assessed separately based on its specific usage conditions.
Does the guarantee cover delivery damage?+
Yes. If you receive a damaged seed bag, leaking bottle, or any product with a broken seal, report it immediately via WhatsApp with a photo. We replace or refund the product at no cost within 48 hours.
